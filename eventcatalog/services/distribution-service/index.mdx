# Distribution Service

The Distribution Service manages the logistics of plasma product distribution, including planning shipments, tracking deliveries, and coordinating with healthcare facilities.

## Responsibilities

- Plan distribution logistics for new products
- Release products for distribution after completion
- Coordinate shipments to healthcare facilities
- Track product location during transit
- Manage temperature monitoring during transport

## Events Consumed

### ApheresisPlasmaProductCreated
**From**: manufacturing-service
**Purpose**: Plan distribution logistics
**Critical**: Yes
**Handler**: `ApheresisPlasmaProductCreatedListener`

### ApheresisPlasmaProductCompleted
**From**: manufacturing-service
**Purpose**: Release for distribution
**Critical**: Yes
**Handler**: `ApheresisPlasmaProductCompletedListener`

## Events Published

- `ShipmentScheduled` - When shipment is planned
- `ProductShipped` - When product leaves facility
- `ProductDelivered` - When product reaches destination
- `TemperatureExcursion` - When temperature monitoring detects issues

## Technical Details

### Technology Stack
- **Runtime**: Java 17
- **Framework**: Spring Boot 3.x
- **Messaging**: Apache Kafka with Avro
- **Database**: PostgreSQL

### Deployment
