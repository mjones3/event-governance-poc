# Quality Service

The Quality Service manages all quality assurance activities for plasma products, including scheduling tests, recording results, and ensuring regulatory compliance.

## Responsibilities

- Schedule quality checks for new products
- Perform final quality verification before distribution
- Track test results and compliance status
- Maintain audit trail for regulatory requirements
- Generate quality reports

## Events Consumed

### ApheresisPlasmaProductCreated
**From**: manufacturing-service
**Purpose**: Schedule quality checks for new products
**Critical**: Yes
**Handler**: `ApheresisPlasmaProductCreatedListener`

### ApheresisPlasmaProductCompleted
**From**: manufacturing-service
**Purpose**: Trigger final quality verification
**Critical**: Yes
**Handler**: `ApheresisPlasmaProductCompletedListener`

## Events Published

- `QualityCheckScheduled` - When tests are scheduled
- `QualityCheckCompleted` - When tests complete
- `QualityCheckFailed` - When products fail quality standards
- `ComplianceVerified` - When regulatory compliance is confirmed

## Technical Details

### Technology Stack
- **Runtime**: Java 17
- **Framework**: Spring Boot 3.x
- **Messaging**: Apache Kafka with Avro
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **Document Storage**: S3 (for test results)

### Deployment
- **Environment**: Kubernetes
- **Replicas**: 2
- **Port**: 8085
